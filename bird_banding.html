About

Students in Organimsal Biology BIOL 115 at Minnesota State University are invited to participate in bird banding demonstrations at the MSUM Regional Science Center each fall.

The demonstrations are provided by Chris Merkord, Assistant Professor in Biosciences at MSUM and Tim Grieves, Assistant Professor in Biology at NDSU.

Weather Cancellation Policy

Our top priority is the health and safety of all involved. This includes both the birds as well as the students, faculty, staff, and other visitors to the bird banding event.

Lightning is a safety hazard for people, and rain or high winds are safety hazards for the birds. In the event of lightning, rain, or high winds a bird banding event will be cancelled.

If the chance of rain or high wind is very high, then the event organizers may cancel the event over a day ahead of time. Two hours before the event start time, the organizers will reassess the weather. If rain or high winds are expect at the event start time, the event will be cancelled for the day.

The event start time is the time the first group of students are scheduled to arrive at the banding center. This is usually between 7:30 AM and 8:00 AM. For example, if banding begins at 7:50 AM, then any cancellation will be posted here by 6:20 AM. Check the signup sheet for the exact start time.

If the event is cancelled, the message at the top of this web page will be updated no later than 90 minutes prior to the event start time.

It is your responsibility to check this web page to see if your event has been cancelled.

FAQ

What should I wear?
Mornings can be very cool, so check the weather and dress appropriately. A jacket, long pants, and close-toed shoes are a good idea.
